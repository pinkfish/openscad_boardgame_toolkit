## About Arkham Divider

[global.arkham-divider.com](https://global.arkham-divider.com/) hosts an online community tool designed specifically for players of the *Arkham Horror: The Card Game*.

* **Custom Card Dividers:** It allows players to generate and print custom dividers to organize their Arkham Horror card collection (campaigns, player cards, investigators, etc.).
* **Multiple Designs:** The tool supports a wide variety of community-contributed styles, including designs that mimic the official "Return To" boxes, horizontal/vertical layouts, and ultra-compact designs.
* **Print Preparation:** It provides features to customize the dividers (like changing text and icons), choose different paper sizes (A4, A3, etc.), and prepare layouts for single or double-sided printing.
